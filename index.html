Welcome to Walla Walla Acupuncture at Advance Health Clinic. I offer personalized healthcare in a welcoming setting. As an acupuncturist (East Asian Medicine Practitioner) and family nurse practitioner, my goal is to provide an in-depth integrative approach to your health and well-being. I believe that healing takes place on many levels and involves body, mind and spirit. My work is to invite and support you to connect within and gain a deeper understanding of your potential for wellness.

As an East Asian Medicine and Family Nurse Practitioner, I utilize traditional western medicine to understand your condition while harnessing the energetic medicine of acupuncture to deepen that understanding. My practice incorporates acupuncture, herbal therapy, as well as other modalities to diagnose and treat a variety of conditions.
